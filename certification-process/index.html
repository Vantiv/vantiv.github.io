Overview

The purpose of the Litle & Co. certification process is to verify that your transaction submissions meet the requirements of the Litle Online API. Certification testing is a required phase of integrating with the Litle processing platform, and during this testing, a Litle Implementation Consultant will work closely with you in support of your efforts. Each Litle transaction type has specific test scenarios that use specific data sets simulating real transactions. The Certification environment responds to each submission with a response, allowing you to verify that you have coded correctly to read the response and store the transaction data returned to you.

Process

  • Upon completion of SDK testing (to the point where you feel comfortable to proceed with the official certification testing), please send an email to implementation@litle.com.
    • If a Litle Implementation Consultant has already been engaged, please work directly with the assigned consultant to begin the process
  • Within the email, please specify the following:
    • Merchant or Partner Name
    • Whether or not you have engaged with Litle’s Sales, Business Development or Partner departments
      • If yes, please provide the name of your Litle contact
        • No worries if you have not yet engaged with Litle outside of the SDK testing, we just want to ensure that we are not duplicating any efforts
    • Contact information for your primary technical resource(s) for the certification phase (e-mail address and phones number)
  • A Litle Implementation Consultant will be assigned to work with you in support of your certification testing efforts
    • The Implementation Consultant will be your primary point of contact going forward during the certification testing. Please address all questions, concerns, etc. with your Implementation Consultant
  • A test account will be configured within the Litle certification environment for your use to perform the certification tests
    • The Implementation Consultant will work with you to have the account properly configured, and will provide you with the necessary access credentials
  • Specific certification test cases are provided within section 2.4 of the Litle XML Reference Guide
    • While all certification test cases are available for your use, you are only required to certify for those transactions that you will be processing in Production
    • Additional test cases, which you are free to use at any time, are provided within section 2.5 of the Litle XML Reference Guide
  • Once you have submitted the required certification test cases, the Implementation Consultant will review the cases for completeness and accuracy
    • If any issues are found, the consultant will provide assistance, and work with you to rectification
  • Upon successful completion of certification testing, the Implementation Consultant will provide you with an official certification email specifying details as to what has been certified, i.e. transaction types, connectivity, communication protocols, etc.

Next Steps

The successful completion of certification testing signifies the integration’s technical readiness to transition to Production processing with Litle & Co. There are, however, many other factors that need to be addressed and finalized prior to the commencement of Production processing, including contract finalization, merchant account development, fulfillment of Litle’s PCI compliance requirements, etc. Your Litle Implementation Consultant will be able to provide direction with regards to next steps towards Production processing.
