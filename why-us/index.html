Our Payments Integration does the heavy lifting so that you don’t have to spend time coding the integration to Litle & Co.   Our Integration Platform consists of a suite of language specific SDK’s.  Install the SDK for your development language per our instructions and start sending a transactions to Litle.  Quickly and easily test using our sandbox environment with no sign up or restrictions to work around.

The Litle SDK's allow you to utilize all of Litle's value added features including:

  • Vault
  • Alternative Payments
  • Insights
  • Automatic Account Updater
  • and more
