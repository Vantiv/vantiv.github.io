Security

Security

Litle & Co. is a PCI DSS Level 1 compliant processor. You can verify this by visiting http://www.visa.com/splisting and searching for “litle”. Litle’s first Report on Compliance (RoC) was issued to Visa in 2003, prior to the incorporation of the PCI SSC (Payment Card Industry Security Standards Council) in 2004. This initial independent certification of Litle’s security architecture coincided with our first production transaction processing for early adopters.

Litle doesn’t stop with PCI DSS compliance in our quest for security. We are committed to going well beyond this standard and use it as a baseline for maintaining the highest level of secure operations in the payment processing industry. Security is integral in everything we do, from Engineering to Information Technology to Finance, Legal, Risk, Compliance, and employee awareness. Litle is proud to continue a longstanding heritage of actively participating in an unprecedented transformation of the information security landscape over the past 10 years. Our Chief Security Officer is the Litle founding Chief Technology Officer and has helped create a culture within Litle that continuously leverages security to build a world class enterprise. Our security team members all are CISSP certified and hold numerous other certifications. Our company is a member organization in leading security organizations and councils. Litle also maintains AICPA SSAE16 SOC1 compliance, NACHA compliance, and is independently audited by our acquiring banks and large scale merchants.

Secure Transmission

Litle’s SDK’s utilize the standard HTTPS protocol which provides secure and encrypted communication between your company and Litle. Litle forces HTTPS connectivity in all our environments. For more information on HTTPS click here.

Secure Storage

All of our cardholder data is encrypted and stored in our systems which comply with the highest standards of data protection.

Secure Processing

Our developers have engineered secure methods for ensuring cardholder data during processing. Litle has direct connections with Card Associations that follow their stringent security standards. And, Litle has a tokenization solution that can help customers manage their own PCI DSS compliance in a cost effective and secure manner.
